Football

Rules and how to play

In a football match, there are eleven people on each team with seven people on the bench to eventually come on and play later on in the match. To win, your team needs to score more goals than the opposing team.
To score, someone on the pitch needs to kick the ball past the other team's goalkeeper into the goal. The match lasts 90 minutes and whoever scores the most goals by the end of the 90 minutes, win!

Leagues

In football, there are 210 different proffessional men football leauges in many seperate countries. Each country that has a league, has more than one league. Each one is called a division. After the football season, the bottom three teams in the league will get relegated to the lower league, or division. The more times you get relegated, the higher divisipon, and the lower the division, the better.
The leagues mentioned under are the top five leagues, (in order)

  1. Premier League (England)
  2. La Liga (Spain)
  3. Serie A (Italy)
  4. Bundesliga (Germany)
  5. Ligue 1 (France)
